Some Hard Facts

  • More than half of the customers lapse their policy within the first 5 years of buying them
  • The private life insurance industry paid a staggering INR 56,000 crs and LIC paid INR 71,000 crs of surrender benefit in the year FY’20
  • This is a growth of 15% over the previous year and almost 70% of the new business premium that they collected for the year

We understand that low persistency has been plaguing the life insurance industry for decades. While the focus on persistency has increased recently within the industry, no one has really cracked it!

Today’s persistency management solutions, especially the outsourced activities, follow a one size fits all approach, where the scope of work is generally limited.

To overcome these challenges, we’re building a company with an endeavour to provide a comprehensive solution fully dedicated to the life insurance industry. Our persistency management solution comprises of three key pillars:

  • Customer retention through embedded value prioritised renewal calling using advanced analytics. In-built rewards mechanism for timely premium payment by policyholders.
  • Marketplace for Policy loan distribution
  • Industry’s first platform for Policy assignment
